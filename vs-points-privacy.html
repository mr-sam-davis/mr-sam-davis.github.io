Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Points ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app on your iOS device.

Information We Collect

Device Identifier

When you perform a search, the app records your device's Vendor Identifier (identifierForVendor) — a UUID assigned by iOS to identify your device uniquely for this app. This identifier resets if you uninstall and reinstall the app, and is not shared with other apps.

Search Queries

Each search you perform is logged with the following information:

  • Origin airport (3-letter IATA code, e.g. LHR)
  • Destination airport (3-letter IATA code, e.g. JFK)
  • Month of travel searched
  • Device identifier (as described above)
  • Timestamp of the search

Flight Availability Data

When the app fetches reward seat availability, the results (points costs and seat availability by cabin class for a given route and date) are cached. This data is not linked to you personally.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any other personal contact details
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Payment or financial information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history beyond searches made within this app
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access

How We Use This Information

Search query logs (including device identifier) are used solely to understand aggregate usage patterns — for example, which routes are searched most often. This helps us maintain and improve the app.

Cached flight availability data is used to avoid redundant network requests and to serve faster results to all users.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google)

The app uses Firebase Firestore, a cloud database provided by Google LLC, to store cached flight data and search query logs. Data is transmitted to and stored on Google's servers. Google's privacy practices are described at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Firebase Analytics is disabled in this app. No advertising identifiers are used.

Virgin Atlantic

To retrieve reward seat availability, the app opens a hidden browser session and makes requests to Virgin Atlantic's website (virginatlantic.com) on your behalf. These requests include the route and date you searched. Virgin Atlantic's own servers will process these requests, and their privacy policy applies to any data they collect as a result.

Data Retention

Search query logs and cached flight availability data are stored in Firebase Firestore. We do not apply an automatic deletion schedule at this time. You may contact us (see below) to request deletion of data associated with your device identifier.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
